10 Div Cookie Recipe

Bake time: 40 Minutes

Ingredients: 8

These cookies are perfect for special occasions such as christmas, easter, birthdays or making videos that may be used for web-development, especially when you get 10 subscribers and you have to make a website with 10 divs!

Trivia!

What is the hottest part of this oven?

2 Cups Flower

1 1/2 Cups Sugar

1 Cup Butter

1 Cup Chocolate Chips

1 tsp Salt

1 tsp Vanilla

1 tsp Baking Soda

1 Egg

Step 1:

First things first, mix the flower, sugar, butter, chocolate chips, salt, vanilla, baking soda, and egg into a bowl until it forms a thick dough.

Step 2:

Grab some cling wrap, and roll the dough into a ball, and cover the dough with the cling wrap, put it in the fridge for 20 minutes.

Step 3:

Grab the dough from the fridge, then roll it into balls. Place onto a cookie sheet and preheat the oven to 180°C or 350°F then place the cookies in the oven for 10 minutes. After cool and eat!
